Frequently Asked Questions.

Is SkillSync right for my family?

The three commitments we make to every family are that we take kids seriously, we make math and literacy the cornerstones of learning, and we give students the resources, structure, and space to pursue their interests. If these commitments resonate with you, SkillSync could be right for your family.

Are there scholarships available?

Yes. SkillSync provides generous financial support, including help securing state scholarships. Additional scholarships are available through the SkillSync Foundation for families that qualify, and we apply for these on your child’s behalf. After submitting an interest form, our Admissions Team will provide a tuition and scholarship estimate.

What does tuition include?

Tuition at SkillSync covers your child’s full school experience, including instruction, learning materials, technology. The only additional costs are the one-time enrollment fee and uniforms and aftercare (if you need it).

How much does SkillSync cost?

SkillSync’s goal is to ensure our education is accessible regardless of a family’s financial situation. We accomplish this through state scholarships, often called ESAs, non-profit funding partnerships, and out-of-pocket tuition. The cost to you is determined primarily by your state of residence. In some states, it will additionally depend on your household income.
