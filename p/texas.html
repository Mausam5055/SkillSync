SkillSync questions, answered
What is SkillSync?
SkillSync is a network of K-8 Microschools in Alabama, Arizona, and Florida. SkillSync recently won the 2024 $1m Yass Prize (often called the ‘Best Picture’ award in education) and we’ve been featured in publications like Forbes, NBC News, Politico, and more.
How are microschools different?
Our Microschools focus on small-group, student-centered learning, ensuring that every SkillSync kid gets the support they need and learns to work collaboratively. Each campus has a unique community where families can feel connected to their child's educational journey.
What is the tuition at SkillSync?
Attending SkillSync is free for every Texas family. Our team will help you secure and utilize a new kind of state scholarship, called TEFA, which will cover 100% of your tuition. Really: there’s no catch!
Is SkillSync right for my family?
The three commitments we make to  every family are that we take kids seriously, we make math and literacy the cornerstones of learning, and we give students the resources, structure, and space to pursue their interests. If these commitments resonate with you, SkillSync could be right for your family.
